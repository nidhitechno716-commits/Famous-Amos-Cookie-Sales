# Famous-Amos-Cookie-Sales
The business needed a clear understanding of its global cookie sales performance across different countries and product variants. Traditional reports were static and lacked time-based insights (MTD, QTD, YTD), making it difficult to track profitability and sales trends.

## Approach & Data Preparation
Dataset sourced from Kaggle (fictional but realistic sales data).
Columns included: Store Location (Country), Product Type, Units Sold, Revenue per Unit, and Cost per Unit
Created a Dynamic Calendar Table to support advanced time intelligence.
Added calculated columns for Profit and Gross Profit Margin (GP%).
Built DAX measures for:
Sales, Units, and Profit (MTD, QTD, YTD)
Same Period Last Year (for YoY analysis)

## Dashboard Features
Global View: Interactive map to visualize revenue distribution by country.
Product Insights: Identified best-selling products (e.g., White Chocolate, Chocolate Chip).
Time Intelligence: Trend analysis across days, months, quarters, and years.
Performance KPIs: Revenue, Units Sold, Profit, GP Margin% displayed as cards
Comparison: YoY analysis with Same Period Last Year DAX functions.

## Key Insights
Revenue & Profit: $9.08M in total revenue with $5.34M profit across 5 years.
Top Product: White Chocolate & Chocolate Chip consistently dominated sales and profits.

Time Trend: Strong revenue growth in Q4, indicating seasonal demand peaks.
Weekday Patterns: Highest revenue observed on Tuesdays & Mondays, with a dip towards weekends.
Germany Deep Dive: Chocolate Chip drove 60% GP margin with December being the peak month.

## Impact 
usiness leaders gained real-time insights into sales performance
